        Pysiotherapy

        Our physiotherapists begin each patient's journey with a comprehensive assessment, carefully evaluating their physical condition, mobility, and functional abilities. This assessment forms the foundation of a tailored treatment plan designed to address specific needs and goals.

        Know More

        Occupational Therapy

        Our occupational therapists begin by conducting thorough assessments, considering the physical, cognitive, emotional, and environmental factors that influence an individual's occupational performance. These assessments serve as the basis for developing personalized treatment plans that address specific goals and aspirations.

        Know More

        Rehab Therapy

        For patients recovering from surgeries, our rehab therapists provide specialized care to promote healing and ensure a smooth transition back to daily activities. They guide patients through targeted exercises and therapies to optimize post-surgical recovery.

        Know More

        Cognitive Therapy

        Cognitive therapy is a goal-oriented approach that focuses on identifying and challenging negative thought patterns and beliefs that may contribute to emotional distress and behavioral issues. Our cognitive therapists work collaboratively with patients to reframe these thoughts and develop healthier coping strategies.
